  • Cybersecurity refers to the practice of protecting computer systems, networks,
    and data from unauthorized access, attacks, and damage.
    It encompasses various measures, technologies, and processes designed to
    safeguard information and prevent cyber threats.
    Key objectives of cybersecurity include maintaining the confidentiality, integrity,
    and availability of data and systems.
    Cybersecurity involves the identification and mitigation of vulnerabilities and
    risks, such as malware,hacking, phishing, and social engineering attacks.
